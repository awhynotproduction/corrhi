# Community Garden

A shared garden space in the neighborhood. Not just about growing food — it's about creating a reason for neighbors to be in the same place regularly.

## Why

Most community-building efforts start with events. Events are one-off. A garden is ongoing — it gives people a reason to show up repeatedly, and the work itself creates natural conversation. [[notes/shared-meals-as-ritual|Shared meals from shared gardens]] close the loop.
